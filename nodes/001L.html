Relations et carrés exacts / 1. Exactness in the 2-category $\Cat$ [001A] /
[001L] 1·a.

We denote the bicategory of bimodules (or profunctors, or distributors) as $\Prof$, and its composition [of 1-cells, NDR] as $\otimes$.
